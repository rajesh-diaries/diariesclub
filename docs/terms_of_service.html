8. Play sessions

  • A session is a fixed, prepaid block of play time at the venue. Standard durations are 60 minutes or 120 minutes, with longer durations introduced from time to time at our discretion.
  • A grace period of around 10 minutes is included before any overrun is treated as an extension.
  • Once you start a session by completing the staff QR scan at the venue, the session is considered begun and the corresponding wallet amount is non-refundable.
  • If you cancel a session before the QR scan, you receive a full refund to your wallet automatically.
  • Extensions are paid and start counting from your original session expiry — i.e., grace-period overrun is deducted from any extension you buy, so paid time isn't doubled up.
  • If you do not arrive at the venue after creating a session, the session auto-cancels by a server cron and the wallet amount is returned to your balance.
  • We may pause, reduce, or extend a session at venue-staff discretion in case of emergencies (medical, safety, fire alarm, etc.).
