1. What this policy covers

This Refund Policy explains when and how you can get a refund for money you have spent through the Diaries Club mobile app — wallet top-ups, play sessions, food and FIT orders, session extensions, and workshop registrations.

This policy is part of the contract between you and Planovative Diaries LLP ("Diaries Club," "we," "us"). It should be read together with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Birthday parties are not covered by this policy. Birthday party deposits and final payments are arranged offline between you and our team, not through the app. The refund rules for a confirmed birthday party are set out in the written confirmation we send you at the time of booking.

2. The wallet — quick recap

The Diaries Club wallet is closed-system prepaid store credit — the digital equivalent of a café gift card. It is usable only at Play Diaries / Diaries Club venues, for play sessions, food and drink, session extensions, and workshops. The wallet is not a bank account: it cannot be withdrawn as cash, transferred to a bank, or sent to another person.

Full wallet rules are in Terms of Service §7. The most important consequences for refunds are:

  • Most refunds go back to your wallet, instantly.
  • A refund to your original payment method (card, UPI, etc.) is possible only within 7 days of the original top-up, via our payment partner Razorpay, and only on customer request to support. After 7 days, even a "to source" request can only be paid as wallet credit.
  • Top-up bonuses (e.g., ₹300 free on ₹3,000) are non-withdrawable and non-transferable — they cannot be refunded to your card/UPI under any circumstances.

4. How refunds are paid

4.1 Wallet refunds (the default)

A refund to your Diaries Club wallet is instant — the money is back in your balance within seconds of the refund being approved. You can see it under Profile → Wallet history with the transaction type session_refund or order_refund.

4.2 Refunds to your original payment method (card / UPI / etc.)

A refund to source is processed through Razorpay, our payment partner.

  • You may request a refund to source in cases of (a) a wallet top-up that was wrong, accidental, or unauthorised, or (b) wallet balance left at the time we terminate your account in your favour (see Terms of Service §15.3).
  • The request must be made within 7 days of the original top-up — this is Razorpay's standard reversal window. Beyond 7 days, refunds can only be paid as wallet credit.
  • Once approved, Razorpay typically credits your card or UPI account within 5–7 working days. This timeline is set by Razorpay and your bank; we cannot accelerate it.
  • We send you an in-app and SMS confirmation when we initiate the refund.

4.3 Non-refundable or partial-refund situations

Some scenarios are deliberately non-refundable or limited: - A play session that has already started (the QR scan was completed) — even partially used time, because we have already reserved staff and play-area capacity for you. - An order whose preparation has already started in the kitchen — once cooking has begun, ingredients are committed. - Quality complaints for food, drink, or meal orders that are not raised at the venue before you leave — we no longer have the order to inspect. - Workshop registration cancelled 24 hours or more before the start time is eligible for a 50% refund only — this lets us part-pay our instructors regardless of last-minute drop-offs. - Workshop registration cancelled less than 24 hours before the start time is non-refundable. - Hero cards, XP, perks, and other gameplay artefacts. - Top-up bonuses (the "₹300 free on ₹3,000" portion is a promotional gift, not money you paid).

5. How to request a refund

5.1 Most refunds happen automatically

If your scenario is covered by the table in §3 with an "automatic" outcome (session cancelled before scan, workshop cancelled by us, order cancelled before kitchen preparation starts, etc.), the refund is issued by our server or by venue staff without you needing to do anything further.

5.2 Quality complaints — must be raised at the venue

For food, drink, FIT meal, or combo orders, a quality refund must be raised at the venue before you leave. Speak to any Diaries Club staff member; they can verify the issue and issue the refund on the spot via the admin console. We are unable to action quality complaints once you have left the venue, because we no longer have the order to inspect.

6. Disputes and escalation

If you are unhappy with the outcome of a refund decision:

  1. First, ask us to reconsider by writing to the Grievance Officer (see §7). We will re-examine the case with fresh eyes and respond within 7 working days.
  2. If still unresolved — you may escalate to the appropriate consumer forum under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, or initiate the dispute resolution process described in Terms of Service §24 (informal discussion → Hyderabad arbitration → courts at Hyderabad).
  3. For payment-side disputes with your card or UPI provider — you may also raise a chargeback through your bank. Please note that initiating a chargeback for a top-up you have in fact spent is treated as a breach of our Acceptable Use policy and can result in account suspension or termination (Terms of Service §14, §15).

You retain your statutory rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 — nothing in this Refund Policy limits your right to approach a consumer forum.
